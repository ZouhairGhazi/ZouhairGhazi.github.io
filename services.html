Venue



FMPTMUN is proud to call the Faculty of Medicine and Pharmacy of Tangier of the Abdelmalek Essaadi University its home.
The beautiful city of Tangier, Morocco, is the northernmost city in the country, its astonishing bay looks onto the mountains of the South of Spain,
as they are separated by a 14 km stretch of sea called the Strait of Gibraltar. Tangier’s strategic geographic setting is one of its most important assets,
and puts it on the threshold of the most pressing political and diplomatic affairs.

Social events



FMPTMUN’s international conference vows to be an exceptional social experience. Since this is the first edition of the FMPTMUN international conference,
it will hold a bouquet of various social events which will make for a fun time for networking and building relationships, along the Tangier bay and also its beautiful Atlantic coast.

About FMPTMUN

FMPTMUN is a model united nations club within the medical college of Tangier, where members get to learn about MUNs and how they work, to eventually participate in international conferences and represent our college and our city.
